Project Brief: Fictional Watch Repair and Maintenance Company

Company Name
ChronoCare Atelier

Business Summary
ChronoCare Atelier is a premium local watch service studio that provides diagnostics, repair, servicing, polishing, and pressure testing for mechanical and luxury watches.

Build Scope
Single marketing website completed in 1 week, production-quality front-end, no complex backend dashboard.

Target Audience
- Owners of luxury and mid-to-high-end watches who need trusted maintenance.
- Professionals (ages 28-60) who value reliability, craftsmanship, and transparent service.
- Collectors seeking specialist handling for mechanical/automatic watches.
- Local customers who prefer a physical drop-off option and clear turnaround times.

Business Goal
Generate qualified service requests and appointment bookings by building trust fast.

Primary CTA
Book a Service Assessment

Secondary CTAs
- Request a Quote
- Call the Workshop

Website Pages
1. Home
   - Hero with trust message and primary CTA
   - Core services snapshot
   - Proof points (years of experience, watches serviced, rating)
   - Process preview
   - Testimonials
   - Final CTA block

2. Services
   - Service categories (Full Service, Battery/Seal, Crystal Replacement, Bracelet Restoration)
   - What is included, starting price ranges, expected turnaround time
   - FAQs tied to service decisions

3. About
   - Brand story and expertise
   - Certifications/credentials
   - Workshop standards and equipment highlights

4. Service Process
   - Step-by-step flow: Intake -> Diagnostic -> Quote Approval -> Service -> QA -> Return
   - Service guarantees and warranty terms

5. Contact / Booking
   - Lead form (name, email, phone, watch brand/model, issue, preferred date)
   - Map/address, business hours, phone, email
   - Clear response-time promise

Tone and Voice
- Precise, calm, expert, and trustworthy
- Premium without being flashy
- Confident and transparent (no hype language)

Visual Direction (Premium Look)
- Clean editorial layout with strong spacing and alignment discipline
- Color palette: deep charcoal, warm ivory, brushed-gold accent, muted steel gray
- Typography pairing:
  - Serif display for headings (luxury/craft signal)
  - Modern sans-serif for body and UI text (clarity/readability)
- High-quality close-up imagery (watch details, tools, workshop textures)
- Subtle animations only (fade/slide reveals, soft hover transitions)
- Refined UI details: thin dividers, elevated cards, consistent radius and shadows

Required Features (1-Week Scope)
- Fully responsive layout (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Sticky header with clear CTA access
- Conversion-focused hero and repeated CTA placements
- Service comparison cards with key facts
- Testimonial section with believable social proof
- Booking/contact form with front-end validation and success/error states
- Accessibility baseline:
  - Semantic HTML
  - Keyboard focus states
  - Sufficient color contrast
  - Reduced-motion support
- SEO basics:
  - Page titles/meta descriptions
  - Open Graph tags
  - Structured data for LocalBusiness (if time permits)
- Performance basics:
  - Optimized images
  - Lazy loading where appropriate
  - Font loading strategy

Success Criteria
- UX/Visual Quality
  - Feels like a real premium client website, not a template.
  - Consistent design system across all sections.

- Conversion Readiness
  - Primary CTA appears above the fold and in key decision sections.
  - Contact form is clear, short, and easy to complete on mobile.

- Technical Quality
  - No obvious layout breaks on common viewport sizes.
  - Good Lighthouse outcomes (target):
    - Performance >= 85
    - Accessibility >= 95
    - Best Practices >= 90
    - SEO >= 90

- Content Quality
  - No placeholder text.
  - Service details are specific enough to look credible.

1-Week Execution Plan
Day 1: Finalize brief, sitemap, content outline, and design references.
Day 2: Create wireframes and lock design system tokens (type, color, spacing, components).
Day 3: Build global layout, header/footer, and Home page sections.
Day 4: Build Services, About, and Service Process pages.
Day 5: Build Contact/Booking page and validation states.
Day 6: Premium polish pass (motion, spacing rhythm, hover/focus states, image treatment).
Day 7: QA pass (responsive, accessibility, SEO, performance) and deployment-ready cleanup.
