## Services (`/services`)

### Section 1: Services Hero
- Headline: Specialist watch servicing with transparent scope and pricing
- Intro paragraph: Every watch is first assessed, then quoted. You approve the work before service begins, so you always know what is being done and why.

### Section 2: Detailed Service Cards

#### Service Card 1
- Service name: Full Mechanical Service
- Best for / symptoms: Irregular timekeeping, reduced power reserve, overdue maintenance, dry movement behavior.
- Included work list:
  - Full movement disassembly and inspection
  - Ultrasonic cleaning of movement components
  - Lubrication to manufacturer-grade standards
  - Reassembly, regulation, and timing checks
  - Case and gasket inspection
- Not included note: Replacement crowns, crystals, and major component fabrication are quoted separately if required.
- Starting price range: GBP 320-GBP 690
- Typical turnaround range: 10-15 working days
- Card CTA: Request Quote

#### Service Card 2
- Service name: Quartz Battery and Seal Service
- Best for / symptoms: Stopped watch, intermittent function, moisture concerns after battery life end.
- Included work list:
  - Battery replacement
  - Gasket inspection and replacement where needed
  - Contact point cleaning
  - Water-resistance pressure test (where case design supports it)
- Not included note: Damaged crowns, cracked crystals, and corroded movement modules are scoped separately.
- Starting price range: GBP 85-GBP 160
- Typical turnaround range: 1-2 working days
- Card CTA: Request Quote

#### Service Card 3
- Service name: Crystal Replacement
- Best for / symptoms: Cracked, chipped, scratched, or compromised crystal integrity.
- Included work list:
  - Crystal removal and case-seat preparation
  - New crystal fitment and sealing
  - Alignment and visual finish checks
  - Pressure test where applicable
- Not included note: Dial restoration and hand replacement are not part of this service.
- Starting price range: GBP 140-GBP 340
- Typical turnaround range: 3-6 working days
- Card CTA: Request Quote

#### Service Card 4
- Service name: Bracelet and Case Refinement
- Best for / symptoms: Surface wear, light scratches, uneven finish, clasp stiffness.
- Included work list:
  - Controlled refinishing of case and bracelet surfaces
  - Edge definition preservation checks
  - Clasp adjustment and function check
  - Final clean and visual QA
- Not included note: Deep dents, metal filling, and case laser work are quoted separately.
- Starting price range: GBP 110-GBP 290
- Typical turnaround range: 4-7 working days
- Card CTA: Request Quote

#### Service Card 5
- Service name: Water-Resistance Test and Seal Check
- Best for / symptoms: Post-impact checks, seasonal swim use, uncertain gasket condition.
- Included work list:
  - Dry pressure test
  - Gasket condition review
  - Caseback/crown seal recommendations
  - Written test outcome summary
- Not included note: Full diver certification and manufacturer-authorized re-certification are excluded.
- Starting price range: GBP 65-GBP 120
- Typical turnaround range: Same day to 1 working day
- Card CTA: Request Quote

### Section 4: Service FAQ
- FAQ heading: Common service questions

- Q1: Do you begin work before I approve the quote?
  - A1: No. We assess the watch, send the estimate, and wait for approval before beginning service.

- Q2: What warranty do you provide?
  - A2: We provide a 12-month workmanship warranty on covered service work and replaced service parts.

- Q3: Do you test water resistance after seal-related work?
  - A3: Yes, where the watch case design supports pressure testing. Results are included in your service notes.

- Q4: Do you only use genuine parts?
  - A4: We prioritise genuine manufacturer parts where available. If unavailable, we explain equivalent options before any decision.

- Q5: Can I decline service after diagnostic inspection?
  - A5: Yes. You can decline after receiving your estimate. A diagnostic bench fee may apply depending on complexity.

- Q6: How often should a mechanical watch be serviced?
  - A6: Most mechanical watches benefit from service every 4-6 years, depending on usage and manufacturer guidance.

- Q7: Can you remove all scratches during refinishing?
  - A7: We focus on controlled refinishing that protects case geometry; not every deep mark can be safely removed.

- Q8: Do you service vintage watches?
  - A8: Yes, case by case. We assess parts risk and service suitability before confirming scope.
