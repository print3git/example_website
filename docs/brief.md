# Project Brief: ChronoCare Atelier

## Company Summary
ChronoCare Atelier is a premium local watch service studio providing diagnostics, repair, servicing, polishing, and pressure testing for mechanical and luxury watches.

## Build Scope
Single marketing website completed in 1 week, production-quality front-end, no complex backend dashboard.

## Target Audience
- Owners of luxury and mid-to-high-end watches who need trusted maintenance.
- Professionals (ages 28-60) who value reliability, craftsmanship, and transparent service.
- Collectors seeking specialist handling for mechanical/automatic watches.
- Local customers who prefer a physical drop-off option and clear turnaround times.

## Business Goal
Generate qualified service requests and appointment bookings by building trust quickly.

## Primary CTA
Book a Service Assessment

## Secondary CTAs
- Request a Quote
- Call the Workshop

## Required Pages
1. Home
2. Services
3. About
4. Service Process
5. Contact / Booking

## Tone and Voice
- Precise, calm, expert, and trustworthy.
- Premium without being flashy.
- Confident and transparent (no hype language).

## Visual Direction
- Clean editorial layout with strong spacing and alignment.
- Palette: deep charcoal, warm ivory, brushed-gold accent, muted steel gray.
- Serif display for headings, modern sans-serif for body and UI.
- High-quality close-up workshop/watch imagery.
- Subtle motion only: restrained fades/slides and soft hover transitions.
- Refined UI details: thin dividers, elevated cards, consistent radius and shadows.

## Required Features
- Fully responsive layout (mobile/tablet/desktop).
- Sticky header with clear CTA access.
- Conversion-focused hero and repeated CTA placement.
- Service comparison cards.
- Testimonials.
- Booking/contact form with validation and success/error states.
- Accessibility baseline (semantic HTML, keyboard focus, contrast, reduced motion support).
- SEO basics (titles, meta descriptions, Open Graph).
- Performance basics (optimized images, lazy loading, font loading strategy).

## Success Criteria
- Feels like a real premium client website, not a template.
- Consistent visual system across all pages.
- Primary CTA visible above the fold and repeated at decision points.
- Contact form is short and easy on mobile.
- No obvious layout breaks on common viewports.
- Lighthouse targets: Performance >= 85, Accessibility >= 95, Best Practices >= 90, SEO >= 90.
- No placeholder text.
- Service details are specific and credible.

## 1-Week Execution Constraint
Everything in this plan must fit a one-week build and polish cycle.
